Hello, my name is Marija K.

I design and build websites and stuff_
I started off in web development with a degree in Applied Arts, freelance graphic design experience and faded memories of writing code in Delphi some time in the 2000s.


Clarity is a default in my approach although experimentality and eccentricity are more than welcome. I've always enjoyed developing and visualising ideas from other artistic disciplines, finding that unique point in which great differences cross-over and initiate evolution (yes, just like the DNA).


I feel strongly about animal welfare and ethical consumption so I'm particularly interested in working with vegan-friendly, non-profit organizations and small businesses_
